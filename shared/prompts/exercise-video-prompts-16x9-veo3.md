# Prompts de Vídeo 16:9 para os 24 Exercícios no Veo 3 / Flow

Este arquivo foi preparado para uso manual no `Google Flow` com `Veo 3 / Veo 3.1`, priorizando `constância do movimento`, `clareza biomecânica` e `consistência básica de cena`.

## Instruções gerais

- Use `um chat / projeto por exercício` quando quiser testar consistência máxima.
- Assuma `8 segundos por geração`.
- Todos os prompts abaixo já foram pensados para `16:9`.
- O foco principal é `uma repetição clara`, com `início, meio e fim` dentro do clipe.
- Evite acrescentar instruções extras fora do texto pronto.
- Se o Veo perder a execução, use o `fallback opcional` para gerar a imagem inicial no `Nano Banana Pro`, salve essa imagem e depois use `Frames to Video` no Flow.

## Lock global recomendado

- Adult Brazilian woman around 35 years old
- Medium-light tan skin
- Dark brown hair tied in a low ponytail
- Teal sports bra
- Charcoal high-waist leggings
- White training sneakers
- Minimal home workout corner
- Neutral wall
- Light wood floor
- Soft diffused daylight from the left
- Natural lens
- Stable camera
- Purely visual movement demonstration

## Restrições globais recomendadas

Use esta lógica em todos os prompts, já embutida nos textos abaixo:

- no text
- no subtitle
- no captions
- no logo
- no watermark
- no extra people
- no dramatic camera shake
- no wide-angle body distortion

## 1. Agachamento assistido

### Nota de uso

Priorize a leitura do apoio leve na cadeira e a trajetória do quadril para trás. O vídeo precisa deixar claro que a cadeira é referência de equilíbrio, não tração.

### Prompt principal

```text
16:9 horizontal video, full-body static shot, slight 3/4 lateral view at hip-to-waist height, natural lens, level horizon, stable camera.

An adult Brazilian woman around 35 years old with medium-light tan skin, dark brown hair in a low ponytail, teal sports bra, charcoal high-waist leggings, and white training sneakers performs one slow controlled repetition of assisted squat using a simple dining chair for light balance only.

Environment: minimal home workout corner, neutral wall, light wood floor, soft diffused daylight from the left, photorealistic, clean anatomy, natural body proportions, no gym atmosphere.

Movement over 8 seconds: begin standing upright with feet hip-width apart and both hands resting lightly on the top edge of the chair back; then move the hips backward first, soften the knees, and let the torso incline slightly while keeping the heels grounded; finish in a comfortable supported squat depth with the chair still centered in front of the body, chest open, knees aligned with the feet, and no deep athletic squat.

Keep the chair back and both hands fully visible at all times. The movement must read clearly from start to middle to final position in one continuous repetition. Purely visual exercise demonstration focused on biomechanics and support clarity.

Exclusions: no text, no subtitle, no captions, no watermark, no logo, no extra people, no dramatic camera movement, no hidden heel contact, no deep athletic squat, no exaggerated lumbar arch.
```
